Teachers' Involvement and ICT Use in Uganda's Lower Secondary Curriculum

This postgraduate research investigates how teachers are engaging with and implementing ICT tools within Uganda's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) framework. The study examines barriers, opportunities, and best practices for effective ICT integration at the lower secondary level.

Postgraduate Research 2024-2025
Research Study

Influence of NCDC Capacity-Building Programs on CBC Implementation

Exploring how National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) training programs impact teachers' ability to effectively implement the Competency-Based Curriculum with ICT integration.

Community & Student Projects

ICT-based innovations addressing real-world challenges

Digital Learning Corners (DLCs)

Establishing dedicated spaces in schools that improve girls' access to ICT education and digital skills. These corners provide safe, supportive environments where female students can develop confidence in technology.

Key Features:

  • Gender-inclusive ICT training spaces
  • Mentorship programs for female students
  • Access to computers and learning resources
  • Building digital confidence in underserved communities
Empowering Girls in ICT

Farm-Based Management System (CROCONFIG)

A student-developed ICT solution designed to help farmers manage their operations more efficiently. CROCONFIG provides digital tools for crop planning, resource tracking, and harvest management.

System Capabilities:

  • Crop rotation planning and scheduling
  • Resource and expense tracking
  • Harvest records and yield analysis
  • Weather and season planning tools
Supporting Agricultural Innovation

Sickbay Management System

A healthcare management solution designed for school sickbays and small health facilities. This system was recommended for presentation at the COMSTEDA 22 Conference for its practical approach to health record management.

System Features:

  • Patient records and visit tracking
  • Medicine inventory management
  • Symptom and treatment logging
  • Health reports and analytics
Conference Recommended

Environmental Innovation Challenge

ICT-driven initiatives addressing environmental challenges through technology. Students develop solutions for recycling, tree planting campaigns, and clean energy adoption.

Focus Areas:

  • Waste management and recycling tracking
  • Tree planting campaign coordination
  • Clean energy awareness and adoption
  • Environmental data collection and analysis
