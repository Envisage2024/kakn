About KAKN Sites & Andrew Kizito

Educator, Researcher, and Technology Advocate

Professional Background

I am an ICT teacher and educational technology researcher passionate about improving the quality of teaching and learning through ICT integration. With a background in Computer Science and a Postgraduate Diploma in Education, I bring a unique mix of technical expertise and pedagogical skills.

Currently, I am pursuing postgraduate studies in Instructional Design and Technology, focusing on how teachers can effectively use ICTs in Uganda's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

My Vision

To build a digitally literate teaching force in Uganda and Africa that uses ICTs not only for lesson delivery but also for innovation, problem-solving, and lifelong learning.

My Mission

Through KAKN Sites, I strive to:

  • Bridge the gap between teachers and technology
  • Provide affordable ICT training tailored for local contexts
  • Create research-based solutions to improve education
